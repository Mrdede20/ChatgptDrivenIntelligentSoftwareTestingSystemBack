Yes, there is an input which can make the two programs output different results.

Here is an example input:

```
6
5 6 7 6 5 4
7 6 5 4 3 4 5 6 7
3 4 5 4
1 2 3 4 3 2 1
7 6 5 4 3 2 1
```

The first program will output:
```
NO
NO
NO
NO
NO
NO
```

The second program will output:
```
NO
NO
NO
YES
NO
NO
```
